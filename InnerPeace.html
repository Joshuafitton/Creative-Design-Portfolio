Mobile App - UX/UI Research, Design & Branding

Inner Peace

Inner Peace is a mindful meditation app for mobile.

Tools

I

Design Goals

I initiated the design process by outlining the primary objectives for this app.

Provide Effective & High Quality Content.
Engage Users with Aesthetic & Emotional Appeal
Introduce Accessibile & Inclusive Features

II

User Research

I then conducted thorough user research to gain insight into the challenges travelers encounter when organising their trips.

  • Guided Meditation
  • Budget Management
  • Self-Guided Meditation
  • Morning Meditators
  • Evening Meditators
  • Short Sessions
  • Long Sessions
  • Breathing Exercises
  • Mindfulness Practices
  • Focus on Sleep
  • Stress Reduction

Logo Design

I designed the Inner Peace app logo as a lotus flower in a circle to symbolise purity, tranquility, and holistic well-being.
