Mobile App - UX/UI Research, Design & Branding

VOYAGE

Voyage is a travel planning app that simplifies trip organization and consolidates all essential information in one place. It's designed for both solo travelers and group adventures, offering intuitive tools for itinerary management, activity scheduling, and expense tracking.

Tools

I

Design Goals

I initiated the design process by outlining the primary objectives for this app.

Centralise Travel Organisation
Provide Useful Functionalities
Design a Clean User Interface

II

User Research

I then conducted thorough user research to gain insight into the challenges travelers encounter when organising their trips.

Behaviours

  • Frequent Communication
  • Task Delegation
  • Budget Management
  • Research and Compare Options
  • Group Decision Making
  • Addressing Disagreements
  • Flexibility in Planning
  • Setting Deadlines
  • Maintaining Organisation
  • Continuous Feedback
  • Celebrating Milestones
  • Leverage Digital tools

Pain Points

  • Coordination Challenges
  • Communication Issues
  • Budget Discrepancies
  • Travel Logistics
  • Accommodation Conflicts
  • Activity Planning
  • Keeping Track of Details
  • Changing Plans
  • Task Management
  • RSVP Tracking
  • Dealing with Conflicts
  • Accessibility Concerns

Desires

  • Smooth Coordination
  • Centralised Information
  • Cost-Effective Planning
  • Efficient Communication
  • Shared Responsibilities
  • Adjust Plans
  • Safety and Security
  • Customisation of activities
  • Clear Itinerary
  • Stress-Free Planning
  • Inclusive Planning
  • Memorable Experiences

IX

Logo Design

I designed the primary logo for the app with a focus on simplicity and clarity. Opting for a minimalistic compass design not only symbolizes navigation and exploration but also aligns with the app's core functionality of aiding users in planning their travels.

X

Prototype

I then infused my style guide into the mockup to create a high-fidelity prototype, ensuring that my portfolio's design was consistent, visually appealing, and aligned with my personal brand.

XI

Wireframing

Upon completing a stylised prototype, I transitioned to wireframing my portfolio design, laying the foundational structure and functionality to ensure a seamless and intuitive user experience.

XII

Final Design

I implemented the research insights to construct a fully functional prototype of the Voyage App using Adobe XD, ensuring seamless integration of user-centered design principles and addressing the specific needs identified during the research phase.
